Interesting facts about a Camel

  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  • When a camel finally does find water, it can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  • Mother camels carry their calves up to 14 months before giving birth

Interesting facts about a Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
